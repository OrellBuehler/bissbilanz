/weight — Enhanced Weight Page

Existing weight chart + log stays at top. New sections below:

📊 Existing: Weight chart (moving avg + projection) · Weight log form · History list
NEW — Caloric Lag Detector
Auto-detected lag shown as "Your weight responds to eating ~3 days later" with correlation chart showing each offset. Slider to override.
NEW — Macro Impact on Weight
Bar chart: correlation of each macro (protein/carbs/fat/fiber) with weight change rate. E.g. "Higher protein days correlate with -0.05kg/day"
NEW — Meal Timing & Weight
Scatter plot: eating window width vs. weight change. "Days with eating window <10h correlate with weight loss"
NEW — Micronutrient Gaps
Heatmap: nutrient adequacy vs. weight trend. Flags nutrients where deficiency correlates with weight plateaus.

/sleep — New Sleep Page

Brand new page with logging + analytics:

Sleep Log Form
Quick entry: duration (hours:minutes picker) + quality slider (1-10). Expandable "more details" for bedtime, wake time, wake-ups.
Sleep Trend Chart
Dual-axis: duration bars + quality line over 7/30/90 days. Moving average overlay.
Food → Sleep Correlations
"Foods that help your sleep" / "Foods that hurt your sleep" — ranked lists with correlation strength. Based on evening meals vs. that night's sleep.
Nutrient → Sleep Matrix
Which nutrients correlate with better/worse sleep? Highlights tryptophan, magnesium, fiber, saturated fat.
Pre-Sleep Eating Window
"You sleep better when your last meal is 3+ hours before bed" — correlation of dinner-to-bed gap vs. sleep quality.
